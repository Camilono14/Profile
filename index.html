about me

Hi guys! I'm Camilo Navarro, I'm 32 years old and I live in California, United States, originally from Colombia. I am a very hard-working boy who, like most, struggles to survive. I'm very interested in learning to develop web pages in order to obtain a great job that gives me something of freedom to dedicate myself to what I like the most, like playing video games or doing some sport.

In Colombia I studied university graduating as an aeronautical engineer in 2013, unfortunately I had to flee my country due to force majeure and now I find myself working as an independent employee for companies dedicated to home delivery, something that defines me is never stop fighting for dreams that you want or never stop fighting for something that makes you happy.

Currently I am married to a beautiful woman, her name is Natalia and we have known each other for more than 6 years, we have been married for a little less than a year but we are like souls that were born to be together, I am really happy with her and I fight day by day to give her what she deserves and that is eternal happiness.
